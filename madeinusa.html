Made in the USA
Cool stuff that is made in the USA. "Made in the USA" does not always mean "every part of the item was made in the USA". It does mean the company is headquartered in the USA and the items are at least assembled in the USA by USA citizens

Existing "Made in USA" sites that are worth looking at

All American
This site makes my site pointless. Just go here.

Cheapism
Quality list of consumer goods made in USA. This will probably get you what you need.

REI Made in USA
A rather long list of products that are made in the USA

Authenticity50
Another list of stuff made in the USA

American Cotton
Various categories of stuff made in USA, compiled by American Cotton
